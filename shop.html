What is FPL Mania?

FPL Mania is a player comparison and a transfer planner FPL tool. It helps managers simulate player transfers similarly to the Fantasy Premier League official website so you can visualize your squad in future gameweeks.

Why should you use FPL Mania?

The tool gathers and organizes all the important information regarding player performance like totals points, expected goals/assists, bonus points and non-penality goals. All data is aggregated from the Fantasy Premier League's official website and Understat.
